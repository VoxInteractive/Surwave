**Can you survive for six minutes?**

Stranded on a strange, desolate planet with thousands of aliens chasing you. They seem hungry. Odds are against you, but you see a beacon of light in the distance that might grant you powers to tip the scales in your favour...

## Controls

- WASD or arrow keys || controller left stick or D-pad: **MOVE**
- Mouse cursor || Controller right stick: **AIM**
- Left or right mouse button || Ctrl or Space key || any controller button: **SHOOT**

## Tips

- Keep moving, keep shooting. You move slower while shooting.
- Collect the dropped gems to buy four different upgrades at altars located at the corners.
- Tier 1 upgrades cost 20 gems and Tier 2 ones cost 40 gems.
- You can upgrade your:
  - Gun damage,
  - Gun to shoot multiple projectiles,
  - Shock wave attack to affect a larger area,
  - Movement speed.

## Notes

The web version may hitch a couple of times when you first start playing. This is due to the performance limitations of the web browser environment. For the best experience, download a desktop build.

For best rendering of this pixel art game in a web browser, switch the game to full screen by clicking on the button at the bottom right corner.
